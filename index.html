Frequently Asked Questions
What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount is ₹50,000. However, certain exclusive opportunities may have higher minimums as specified in the deal details.

How are returns paid out?

Returns are typically paid out monthly directly to your registered bank account. The exact payout schedule is specified in your investment agreement.

Can I withdraw my investment early?

Early withdrawal options are available but may be subject to terms and conditions specified in your agreement, including potential early withdrawal fees. Please contact our support team for specific cases.

How secure is my investment?

All investments are backed by legally binding agreements and appropriate collateral. We conduct thorough due diligence on all opportunities before presenting them to our investors.

What happens at the end of the investment tenure?

At the end of the tenure, your principal amount is returned to your registered bank account along with the final returns. We also provide the option to reinvest in new opportunities.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All fees and charges are clearly outlined in your investment agreement with no hidden costs.
